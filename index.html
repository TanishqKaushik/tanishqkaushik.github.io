ML Enhanced Predictive Modeling for Diabetes Diagnosis

This project aims to revolutionize diabetes diagnosis using data science. By meticulously preparing and analyzing health data, the project unveils vital insights. It classifies individuals based on their BMI, uncovers demographic patterns, and highlights the influence of age, gender, and BMI on diabetes risk. This project empowers early intervention, potentially changing lives.

Statistical & ML Analysis of Medical Cost Predictions

This project's goal is to transform healthcare cost management. With healthcare expenses reaching unprecedented levels, accurate prediction models are vital for health insurers, employers, and patients. This project leverages multiple linear regression to uncover influential factors, such as age, BMI, smoking habits, and more. These insights empower stakeholders to make informed decisions, control costs, and enhance healthcare planning.
